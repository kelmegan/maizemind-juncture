Remote work increases overall productivity because people save time commuting and can focus better at home.

The average commute is about 54 minutes a day, which gives workers more time and energy.

Flexible schedules also improve work-life balance, which can help people do better work.

Companies can save money on office space.

However, remote workers can feel isolated, and managers may worry that collaboration suffers.
